The bored experience podcast is project started by two friends in an effort to quell their boredom. The podcast features its two hosts, Dwayne and Brandon often accompanied by random guests made up of members of their respective friend groups as they discuss a diverse variety of topics expressing their opinions, views and presenting their insight on the world around them. Follow the bored experience podcast every saturday at midnight.
